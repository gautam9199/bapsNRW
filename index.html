Data notes: Confirmed: RWTH Aachen (2,366 Indians — Dec 2024), TU Dortmund (466 — Mar 2025), Berlin total intl students 49,801 / 24.8% of 200,852 (Amt für Statistik Berlin-Brandenburg, WS 2024/25). TU Berlin: 10,000+ foreign nationals enrolled (Shiksha/TU Berlin, WS 2024/25); India estimated as 2nd largest group after China (~18% of intl = ~1,800). All other figures are weighted estimates using DAAD national data (59,419 Indian students, WS 2024/25), regional shares, and university-level international ratios. Berlin region share estimated at ~7% of national Indian total based on Berlin's 24.8% overall international student share × India's national prominence, scaled for German-language requirement barriers at HU/FU. Gender: national DAAD average (~70% male / 30% female). Map: state boundaries are simplified administrative borders from the open-source isellsoap/deutschlandGeoJSON project (derived from DIVA-GIS public data), not survey-grade GIS. Sources: DAAD 2024, Destatis, Amt für Statistik Berlin-Brandenburg, RWTH Aachen, TU Dortmund, TU Berlin, Shiksha, isellsoap/deutschlandGeoJSON.
