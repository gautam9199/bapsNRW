Data notes: Confirmed figures: RWTH Aachen (2,366 Indians — RWTH press release Dec 2024), TU Dortmund (466 — TU Dortmund Mar 2025). All other city estimates are proportionally derived using NRW's ~25% share of Germany's 59,419 Indian students (WS 2024/25, DAAD/Destatis), weighted by each university's size, international student ratio, and English-program availability. FernUniversität Hagen has 70,000+ students but is a distance-learning university — Indian enrollment is relatively low given German-language requirements. Gender split follows DAAD national average (~70% male, 30% female). Small cities (Hamm, Iserlohn, Steinfurt etc.) have smaller institutions and therefore lower absolute Indian student counts. Sources: DAAD India 2024, Destatis, RWTH Aachen, TU Dortmund, Federal Statistical Office Germany, university international offices.
